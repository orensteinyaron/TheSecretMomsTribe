# Moving Images — Style Guide

## Visual World
Each video has a consistent "visual world" — setting, lighting, color palette, textures. Not shot-by-shot, but atmospheric.

## Photography Rules
- **NO faces** — hands, backs of heads, over-shoulder, feet, silhouettes only
- **70%+ parent images should show mothers**
- Warm, golden-hour lighting always
- Real, lived-in environments (kitchens, living rooms, parks)
- Editorial photography feel, never stock or AI-looking

## Brand Filter (applied to all photos)
- Resize to 1080x1920
- Gentle desaturation (saturation 0.8)
- Slight brightness lift (1.03)
- NO hue shift
- JPEG quality 88

## Pexels Query Safety
- NEVER: crying, meltdown, distress, medical, studio photos
- NEVER: seasonal/holiday, religious ceremonies, extreme close-ups
- Queries must be concrete: "mom kneeling child eye level living room" not "parenting"

## Ken Burns Motion Types
1. Slow zoom in (1.0 → 1.15)
2. Slow zoom out (1.15 → 1.0)
3. Pan left to right
4. Pan right to left
5. Diagonal drift
6. Subtle float

## Caption Style
- Word-by-word karaoke reveal
- Pink highlight (#E91E8C) on active word
- Text shadow for readability on photos
- No frosted glass panels

## Brand Colors (per pillar)
- parenting_insights: purple/pink
- ai_magic: dark navy/pink
- mom_health: purple/soft pink

## Watermark
- "smt" text bottom-right corner
- Small, semi-transparent
