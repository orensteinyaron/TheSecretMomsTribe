# SMT Content Strategy

## Core Philosophy

- **Never show the process. Show the magic.**
  The output, not the prompt. The result, not the tool.
- **Hook in 0-3 seconds. Magic in 3-10. Payoff in 10-15.**
- **Content mix:** 60% Wow / 30% Trust / 10% CTA
- **Cadence:** 3 posts/day TikTok, 1 post/day IG
- **Aesthetic:** Model B (no face) → evolve to Model A later
- **Voice:** Warm, insider, like a smart mom friend who knows things

---

## Content Pillars (Full Mom Journey)

### 1. Baby & Toddler Magic (ages 1-4)
Bedtime stories, sleep routines, developmental milestones,
first foods, tantrums

### 2. The School Years (ages 5-10)
Homework help, meal planning, activity ideas, learning hacks,
reading and math support

### 3. Tween Territory (ages 10-13)
Body changes, friendship drama, screen time, confidence,
hard conversations

### 4. Teen Survival (ages 13-16)
Talking to teens, mental health, social media, independence,
college prep

### 5. Mom's Mental Load
Organization, reducing overwhelm, self-care, the invisible
mental labor of motherhood

---

## What Goes Viral for This Audience

- Visual AI outputs (personalized stories, images, meal plans
  shown on screen)
- Zero prompting required — one input, instant magic
- Emotional payoff — pride, relief, connection, humor
- Shareable to other moms within seconds of seeing it

---

## Content Format Priority (by platform algorithm)

### TikTok
- Original audio > trending sound > talking head
- Native-feel content outperforms polished production
- 3 posts per day target
- Short-form: 15-60 seconds optimal

### Instagram
- Reels > Carousels > Static
- Reel-focused accounts grow 47% faster
- Keywords in captions now matter more than hashtags for discovery
- 1 post per day target (Reel-first)

---

## Key Insight from Baseline Data

**Meme/relatable content outperforms educational content 25:1.**

The two meme-style posts (50-53 likes) massively outperformed
the 7-part educational series (1-3 likes each). The audience
rewards emotional resonance, not structured teaching.

**Implication:** Lead with Wow and emotion. Wrap any educational
value inside an emotional hook — never lead with the lesson.

---

## Content Type Definitions

### Wow (60% of content)
AI-generated magic outputs that make moms say "I need this."
Examples: personalized bedtime story, custom meal plan,
AI-generated conversation script for tough talks.

### Trust (30% of content)
Relatable memes, shared experiences, "you're not alone" moments.
Builds community. Gets shares. Demonstrates insider knowledge.

### CTA (10% of content)
Direct calls to action — follow, share, save, visit link.
Only after Trust and Wow have built the relationship.
